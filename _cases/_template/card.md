## The Six (intent capture — required to leave `qualifying`)

1. **intent:** what they're actually trying to do (one sentence in their own words if possible)
2. **budget_range:** $X – $Y. Note if pre-approved and with whom.
3. **timeline:** target close date or window. Note urgency drivers.
4. **location:** neighborhoods, zip codes, or "anywhere in metro Austin"
5. **motivation:** the why (relocating, growing family, downsizing, investment, life change)
6. **constraints:** schools, commute, must-haves, must-not-haves, deal-breakers
